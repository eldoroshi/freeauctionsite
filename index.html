Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to create an account?

No! You can create and launch an auction display without signing up. Just add your items and go. Create an account only if you want to save displays for later.

How do I display it on a TV or projector?

Open the display link in any web browser on the device connected to your TV or projector. Then press F11 for fullscreen mode. That's it!

Can I update bids from my phone?

Yes! You get a separate control panel link. Free accounts: Control panel must be opened on the same device as the display (uses local storage). Pro/Event accounts: Control from any device with real-time sync across all screens.

Does it handle payments or bidding?

FreeAuctionSite is focused on the display only ‚Äî showing items and current bids beautifully on screen. For mobile bidding and payments, we integrate with popular auction platforms.

What if my internet goes down during the event?

The display will continue showing the last loaded data. Once internet returns, it automatically reconnects and syncs.

Can I customize the colors to match our event?

Free accounts get our default theme. Pro and Event accounts can customize colors, add logos, and match your event branding.

What is silent bidding mode?

Silent bidding mode (Pro/Event plans) allows bidders to place bids privately through their phones without displaying them on the screen. Perfect for creating excitement when you reveal the final bids at the end of your event.
