Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to create an account?

No! You can create and launch an auction display without signing up. Everything is saved locally in your browser. Create an account only to save displays across devices.

How do I display it on a TV or projector?

Click "Launch Display" and a fullscreen view opens in the same browser. Then drag the window to your TV screen or press F11 for full-screen mode. That's it!

Can I update bids from my phone?

Yes! Keep the tool open on your phone. Update bids in the item list and the display updates instantly ‚Äî they share data in real time via your browser's local storage.

What does the countdown timer do?

When enabled, each item card shows a progress bar that counts down from your set duration. It turns amber at 30% remaining and flashes red at 10% ‚Äî creating urgency for bidders.

What if my internet goes down during the event?

The display continues showing the last data since everything runs locally in your browser. No internet is required after the page loads.

Can I customize the colors to match our event?

Free accounts use our default theme. Pro and Event accounts can customize colors, add logos, and match your event branding.
